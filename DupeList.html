What Is a Dupe?

In Jailbreak trading, a duped item refers to a copy of a limited or retired vehicle created through glitches or exploits that should not normally exist in multiple copies. Duped vehicles have the same original owner ID as another copy, which is why they are identified as duplicates in community databases and value lists.

⚖️

Why It Matters

Because duped items increase the effective supply, they are generally worth less than their clean counterparts in the trading market. Usually duped items sell for a different price and different demand than clean items, and many traders prefer clean items over duped ones, leading to lower prices for duped items compared to clean ones. This is because duped items can be more common and less desirable to collectors, making them less valuable than clean ones, even though owning a duped item doesn’t affect gameplay and doesn’t get removed by the game itself.

🛡️

How to Trade Wisely

Before you trade, check an item’s legitimacy using trusted dupe lists or tools like dupe finders on community value sites. Review the original owner’s username and item history to avoid overpaying for a duped copy if you’re focused on high-value trading.
