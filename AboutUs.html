Who We Are

Built by traders,
for traders.

Jailbreak Exchange is a community-driven platform for Roblox Jailbreak players who want fair, transparent, and informed trading. We built the tools we wished existed — and we keep them free.

In a game where item values shift daily and misinformation spreads fast, our goal is simple: give every player accurate data so they can trade with confidence, not guesswork.

📊 Item Values

Regularly updated values for every Jailbreak item — based on real demand, rarity, and live market trends. No manipulation, no agenda.

🔄 Trade Calculator

Our smart verdict engine goes beyond raw value. It factors in demand tiers, upgrade premiums, and stack bonuses to tell you if a trade is actually a win.

🧬 Dupe Detection

Look up any Roblox account to see known duped items. Our database is community-sourced and updated constantly so you never get caught off guard.

🗄 Hoard Manager

Mark items as investment holds so the calculator knows you're playing long-term. Demand penalties are skipped for hoarded items.
