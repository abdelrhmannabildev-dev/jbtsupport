About Us

Welcome to Jailbreak Exchange. Jailbreak Exchange is a community-driven platform built for players of Roblox Jailbreak who want fair, transparent, and informed trading.

In a game where item values constantly change, misinformation can easily lead to bad trades. Our goal is simple: give players accurate data so they can trade with confidence.

📊 Item Values

We provide regularly updated values for Jailbreak items based on demand, rarity, and market trends. Whether you're trading vehicles, cosmetics, or limited items, our value list helps you understand what your items are really worth.

🔄 Trade Calculator

Our Trade Calculator instantly tells you whether a trade is a win, fair, or loss. No guesswork — just input the items on both sides and get a clear breakdown before you trade.

🧬 Dupe Detection

Duped items can cause serious issues in trades. We track items that are suspected or confirmed dupes, helping players avoid risky trades and protect their inventories.
