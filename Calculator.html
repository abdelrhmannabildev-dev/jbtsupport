Trade Calculator

Smart verdict based on value, demand, upgrade factor, and stack bonuses.

💰
Item Value

Every item has a market value. The calculator starts with the raw difference between what you give and what you get.

📈
Demand

High demand items are easier to re-trade, so they're worth more in practice. Low demand items are harder to move, which lowers their real value. This is factored into both sides of the trade.

⬆ Upgrade OP
Upgrade Tax

When you consolidate many items into one, the person receiving multiple items needs a small premium for the extra work of re-trading them. This is a normal ~500k overpay. Same logic applies in reverse for downgrade trades.

📦
Hoard Mode

If you're holding an item as a long-term investment, mark it in My Hoard. You can set your own personal value and demand — the calculator uses your estimate instead of the market rate, so a low-demand item you believe in won't drag your verdict down.

📦×8
Stack Bonus

Offering 3 or more identical high-demand items gives a small bundle bonus — collectors often pay a slight premium for stacks.

⚖️
Verdict Zones

The bar shows where your trade lands: Big Loss · Loss · Fair · Win · Big Win. Fair means the trade is balanced after all factors. Win means you come out ahead.
